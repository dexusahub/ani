Overview

The Bergara B-14 Ridge is the standard-bearer of the B-14 hunting series — built in Bergara, Spain using the same precision barrel-making technology that supplies custom rifle builders across Europe and North America. It delivers production-rifle affordability with near-custom accuracy thanks to the legendary Bergara barrel, a proprietary two-lug B-14 action, and a glass-fiber-reinforced polymer stock engineered for real-world hunting conditions.

The B-14 action uses a two-lug system with a sliding plate extractor and a coned bolt nose and breech — ensuring smooth, positive feeding and extraction under field conditions. The barrel is 4140 CrMo steel, finished in Graphite Black Cerakote, with a #5 contour and threaded 5/8×24 muzzle. Solid epoxy pillars are permanently molded into the stock for repeatable, stable bedding.

Available in 13 calibers from .22-250 through .300 Win Mag and 7mm PRC. The B14S501C is chambered in .308 Winchester / 7.62×51mm with a 20" barrel and 1:10" twist rate.
