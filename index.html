Why It Stands Out

Key Features

Bergara Barrel

4140 CrMo steel, No. 5 taper, Graphite Black Cerakote® — the same barrel quality trusted by top-tier OEM manufacturers worldwide.

B-14 Action

Super-smooth two-lug system with sliding plate extractor and coned bolt nose for reliable feeding and extraction in any condition.

Sub-MOA Guarantee

Factory guaranteed to produce groups of 1.0 MOA or less at 100 yards using quality factory match-grade ammunition.

Performance Trigger

Bergara curved performance trigger — crisp, clean pull set at approximately 3 lbs from factory. Adjustable 2–5 lbs.

Synthetic Stock

Molded glass fiber reinforced polymer, American-style. Dark gray with black/white speckle and SoftTouch grip coating. Pillar-bedded.

Rem 700 Compatible

Factory drilled and tapped to fit Remington 700-style rings and bases — broad scope mount compatibility. Threaded muzzle 5/8×24.

What Goes With It

Recommended Accessories

Scope Rings & Bases — Remington 700 pattern
Wide aftermarket support: Talley, Leupold, Warne, Burris. 6-48 screw pattern. Standard & 20 MOA options.
Muzzle Brake / Suppressor
5/8×24 threaded muzzle accepts standard .30-cal brakes and suppressors. Direct-thread mount recommended.
Rifle Sling
Standard sling swivel studs installed. Compatible with all two-point hunting slings (Magpul MS1, Blue Force Gear, Vero Vellini).
Aftermarket Triggers
Accepts Remington 700-pattern drop-in triggers. Timney 510, TriggerTech Primary, and Rifle Basix L-2 are popular upgrades.
Bipod
Front sling stud bipod attachment (Harris 1A2-BRM 6–9" recommended for .308 prone shooting).
